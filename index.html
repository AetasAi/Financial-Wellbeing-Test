Is financial pressure showing up in your business?

Financial wellbeing is no longer only a personal issue. For many organisations, it can affect focus, confidence, engagement, retention and overall performance.

This diagnostic helps you take a high-level view of what may already be happening across your workforce, and whether a more structured response could be useful.

1. How confident are you that employees understand the benefits already available to them?
2. How visible is financial stress within your workforce?
3. How often do employees ask for help with pensions, savings, debt, protection or wider money matters?
4. When did you last review your workplace pension and employee benefits?
5. How well does your current wellbeing approach connect with retention, engagement and performance?
6. How easy would it be for your organisation to introduce practical financial wellbeing support?
Please answer all six questions before viewing your result.

Would it be useful to walk through this?

Aetas in the Workplace can help you review what is already in place, identify any gaps, and consider what a proportionate response could look like.

What this diagnostic is designed to reveal

It gives leadership teams a simple way to understand whether employee financial pressure may be creating hidden organisational risk.

The aim is clarity.
Not every organisation needs a complex programme, but most benefit from understanding whether current support is fit for purpose.
